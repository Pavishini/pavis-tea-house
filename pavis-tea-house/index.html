About Pavi's Tea House

A story steeped in passion, tradition and the finest leaves

Our Journey

From Plantation to Your Cup

Founded in 2010 by Pavishini Karthikeyan, a passionate tea connoisseur who traveled the world in search of extraordinary teas, Pavi's Tea House is a celebration of everything that makes tea special.

We believe that every cup of tea is a moment of peace — a chance to slow down, breathe, and savour the beauty of life. Our teas are sourced directly from farmers we trust, ensuring the highest quality while supporting sustainable farming practices.

From the misty hills of Darjeeling to the ancient gardens of Yunnan, every blend we carry has been personally tasted and approved by Pavi herself.
